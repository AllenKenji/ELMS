🏛️ Committee Workflow After Assignment
1.	Scheduling the Meeting
o	The committee chairperson, with the secretary’s support, sets a date and time.
o	Notices are sent to members and sometimes to resource persons or stakeholders.
2.	Deliberation
o	The committee reviews the proposed measure in detail.
o	They may invite subject matter experts, barangay officials, or affected groups to provide input.
o	Amendments or clarifications are discussed.
3.	Committee Report Preparation
o	After deliberation, the committee drafts a report with its recommendation:
	APPROVE (endorse for council readings)
	REVISION (suggest changes before approval)
	REJECTION (recommend not to proceed)
o	This report is signed by the chair and members.
4.	Submission to the Secretary
o	The committee report is formally submitted back to the secretary.
o	It becomes part of the agenda for the next council session.
